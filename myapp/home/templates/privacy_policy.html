Information Security and Data Privacy Policy of ZIPIT.LINK

WHO WE ARE AND WHAT IS ZIPIT.LINK

ZIPIT.LINK is the manufacturer and owner of ZIPIT.LINK (referred to collectively as "Software" or "Service" by us). ZIPIT.LINK is a software for creating and managing online links, supporting various features related to links, affiliate links, shorten links etc.

ZIPIT.LINK respects and commits to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy applies to all ZIPIT.LINK products and services provided. Please read this privacy policy carefully to understand the commitments we make to respect and protect your rights.

LOGIN SECURITY

Directly log into ZIPIT.LINK with your email and password. ZIPIT.LINK requires a minimum of 8 characters for passwords. Unsuccessful login attempts will trigger a 30-second lockout period before users can retry. Passwords are stored as hashes and will never be sent via emailâ€”when creating an account and resetting a password, ZIPIT.LINK will send a link to the email associated with the account to allow users to create a new password.

The complexity of passwords and session length requirements cannot be customized in the application. However, they can be set in the IdP for the SSO execution group.

HOW WE COLLECT INFORMATION

We collect information in two ways: Information we receive from your use of our Service and information you provide directly to us. The information we receive from your use of the services is primarily non-personally identifiable information such as: web browser type, server used, preferred language, queries, timestamps of each access, etc.

We collect non-personally identifiable information to better understand how customers use the services and, if possible, to improve your experience. For example, we collect the time it takes to run login database queries so that we can improve performance.

The information you directly provide to us: The quantity and type of information provided depend on the nature of the interaction. For example, we require customers registering for our services to provide their full name, username, and email address.

Organizations and individuals participating in financial transactions to purchase paid services are required to provide additional information, such as personal and financial information necessary to process transactions. In each case, we only collect the necessary information for the types of interactions.
